How this service helps your team

This service helps product teams, accessibility leads and auditors work more effectively.

A single place for all issues

Record and manage your accessibility issues in one place. This gives your team a clear, actionable backlog to help prioritise and fix problems.

Always-accurate statements

Use your issue data to automatically generate a legally compliant accessibility statement. It's always up to date, with no manual effort.

Better oversight for experts

Accessibility leads can spot common problems to target support. Auditors can add findings directly, saving time and reducing duplicated work.

How it works

From an accessibility issue being found to a live, legally-compliant statement, the process is simple.

  1. Issues are found during testing, an audit or from user feedback.
  2. Issues are added to your product entry in this service.
  3. You update your accessibility statement in your product to point to the unique URL this service provides.
  4. Your statement updates in real-time as issues are added and resolved.

Your statement is always legally compliant and will automatically show if your service is 'fully' or 'partially' compliant based on the issues you've recorded.

Interested in using this service?

This service is currently for teams within the Department for Education.

If you're interested in using it in your department, contact DfE DesignOps at design.ops@education.gov.uk.
